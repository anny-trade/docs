## What is the CFO Anny Line?

The CFO Anny Line is a proprietary trend indicator that classifies market conditions into three states:

- **Accumulate** (`#3ca691` teal green) — Strength. The asset is showing structural strength.
- **Wait** (`#6B6588` muted purple-gray) — Neutral. No clear directional bias.
- **Distribute** (`#B767DE` violet purple) — Weakness. The asset is showing structural weakness.

These states power every tool in the platform — from portfolio overviews to historical scenario analysis.
